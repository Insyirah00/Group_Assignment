History of Eddlee Fried Chicken.

Eddlee Fried Chicken was founded by Mohd Eddlee bin Ismail in 2003 in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. Initially, the business operated as Eddlee Food Services, selling fried chicken at night markets before evolving into a full-fledged restaurant chain.

Eddlee Fried Chicken started its journey in Kelantan, bringing mouthwatering crispy fried chicken to food lovers since its inception.

The brand became popular due to its affordability, crispy texture, and unique spice blend. Today, Eddlee Fried Chicken has 14 branches across Kelantan, including locations like Pengkalan Chepa, Wakaf Che Yeh, Bukit Marak, and Kota Bharu.

With a commitment to quality and affordability, Eddlee Fried Chicken continues to grow and serve customers with its signature flavors.
