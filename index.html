About Us

We are a thriving restaurant chain that are based in Kelantan, Malaysia. Our area of expertise is to prepare and serve crispy and appetizing fried chicken meals to all, with affordable prices.

At Eddlee Fried Chicken, every piece of our chicken is prepared with special marinade and served with a variety of sauces as well as selections of mouth-watering side dishes.

Now, our efforts and support received has made us grown and became one of the top choices by the locals and tourists visiting Malaysia.
